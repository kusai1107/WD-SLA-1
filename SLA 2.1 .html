3. Definitions Input devices of Compputer

Monitor
Displays information in graphical and symbolic forms. It's also known as a visual display unit (VDU).
Speakers
Reproduces sound from a computer. They are essential for watching videos, listening to music, or participating in video calls.
Printer
Transfers text and graphic data from a computer to paper. There are two types of printers: impact and non-impact.

4. Definitions Output devices of Compputer

Mouse
A pointing device that allows users to control the pointer on the computer screen
Microphone
Allows users to input audio into a computer
